Víctor Müller

Bellow is a list of publications I have produced during my doctorate, sorted from most recent to older. Main contributions were to improve artery-vein separation in retinal fundus images, to predict cancer patient survival with the Weibull distribution, and to develop superpixel-based efficient segmentation algorithms.

Publications

  • The topology-overlap trade-off in retinal arteriole-venule segmentation. Juanco-Müller, Á. V., Mota, J. F., Goatman, K. A., \& Hoogendoorn, C. In Medical Imaging 2023: Image Processing (Vol. 12464, pp. 576-586). SPIE.
  • Towards Tumour Graph Learning for Survival Prediction in Head & Neck Cancer Patients. Juanco-Müller, Á. V., Mota, J. F., Goatman, K., \& Hoogendoorn, C. (2022). In 3D Head and Neck Tumor Segmentation in PET/CT Challenge (pp. 178-191). Cham: Springer Nature Switzerland.
  • Deep supervoxel segmentation for survival analysis in head and neck cancer patient. Juanco-Müller, Á. V., Mota, J. F., Goatman, K., \& Hoogendoorn, C. (2021). Deep supervoxel segmentation for survival analysis in head and neck cancer patients. In 3D Head and Neck Tumor Segmentation in PET/CT Challenge (pp. 257-265). Cham: Springer International Publishing.
  • Segmentation of skin lesions by superpixel classification with graph-context CNN.Muller, A. V. J., Hoogendoorn, C., \& Mota, J. F. (2021, July).
